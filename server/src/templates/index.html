Welcome to Tutorme!

Mission

At Community Learning, we strive to give students K-12, the supportive, comfortable and interactive environment that can help them to reach their highest potential both in the classroom and beyond. We give students tutoring that is of the highest caliber, builds confidence and personalized teaching for each of them. As a tutoring service we want our services to be accessible and available for the students. We want to make a difference in each student’s life to give them the resources and support for them to succeed in their academic work. As longhorns, we take care of our community, each other and have each others’ back. We can truly learn together and “succeed together” to make a better future for the next generations. Let’s Go!

Integrity

As tutors, we want to hold each other to the highest degree of ethics and professionalism when providing services to our fellow students. We adhere to being honest and leaders when guiding our peers and building relationships.

Respect

We aim to have high respect for the diversity of our community and we are privileged to provide our services to help make a difference and aspire to provide a supportive learning environment.

Accountability

We accept responsibility for the services we give students on their academic needs and the results. We aspire to provide the highest quality of tutoring to our community.
